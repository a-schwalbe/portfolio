Model logic

How the scores work

Qualifier: The default is 100 AB, matching your setup. You can change it in the top filter.

Normalization: Every key metric is converted into a within-dataset percentile among qualified hitters. Lower-is-better stats such as K%, whiff%, and chase rate are inverted.

Performance Score: Current production is the largest piece, but the score also uses expected stats, contact quality, discipline, and speed so it is not only a batting-average leaderboard.

Breakout Score: Looks for positive gaps such as xwOBA > wOBA, xSLG > SLG, xBA > AVG, low BABIP with strong expected stats, high hard-hit/barrel rates, and acceptable plate discipline. It penalizes players who are already greatly overperforming their expected stats.

Small-sample control: Scores include an opportunity/reliability factor based on PA. With only early-season data, use the score as a ranking signal, not a prediction guarantee.
